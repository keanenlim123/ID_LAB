Seafood Laska

Singapore Laksa, a flavorful and aromatic noodle dish that blends the rich flavors of coconut, spices , seafood. and corn


Ingredients

Laksa Paste:

  • 6 dried chilies (soaked in hot water, seeds removed)
  • 3 fresh red chilies
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 3-4 shallots
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of galangal (optional)
  • 2 stalks lemongrass (white part only, finely chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon shrimp paste
  • 1 tablespoon ground turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Laksa Soup:

  • 200g rice noodles or vermicelli (blanched in hot water)
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1 liter chicken stock
  • 150g prawns (peeled, deveined)
  • 200g fish cakes (sliced)
  • 100g tofu puffs (halved)
  • 1-2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 lime (juiced)
  • Salt to taste

Garnish:

  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Bean sprouts
  • Hard-boiled eggs (halved)
  • Fresh red chilies (sliced)
  • Fried shallots
  • Laksa leaves (Vietnamese coriander) or mint

Steps:

  1. Make the Laksa Paste:
    • Blend the dried chilies, fresh chilies, garlic, shallots, ginger, galangal, lemongrass,
      shrimp paste, turmeric, coriander, and oil into a smooth paste.
  2. Cook the Soup:
    • In a large pot, saut√© the laksa paste over medium heat for 3-4 minutes until fragrant.
    • Add the chicken stock and simmer for 10 minutes.
    • Stir in the coconut milk, fish sauce, sugar, and lime juice. Adjust with salt to taste.
  3. Prepare Noodles and Toppings:
    • Blanch the rice noodles in hot water, then drain.
    • Poach the prawns in the soup for 2-3 minutes until cooked, then set aside.
    • Add the fish cakes and tofu puffs to the soup to heat through.
  4. Assemble:
    • Divide the noodles into bowls.
    • Ladle the hot soup, fish cakes, and tofu puffs over the noodles.
    • Top with prawns, bean sprouts, hard-boiled eggs, coriander, red chilies, fried shallots, and laksa leaves.
  5. Serve:
    • Enjoy your laksa with extra lime and sambal on the side for extra heat.
